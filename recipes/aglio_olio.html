The BEST Lasagna

Description

Inspired by the film "Chef", personalized.

Ingredients

Serves 2-3

  • 1 box of dried spaghetti
  • 1 whole bulb of garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 whole lemon, juiced
  • 1 tbsp red chili flake
  • 1 cup finely chopped parsley
  • 1 cup microplaned parmesean
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, preheat a saucepan on medium heat.
  2. Begin boiling pasta, add olive oil to the saucepan until the oil shimmers.
  3. Once the oil is shimmering, add the garlic and chili flake and slowly stir, do not let it brown.
  4. Once the garlic turns past its shade of blonde, turn off the heat and finish cooking the pasta.
  5. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta water, drain the pasta, and add the pasta back into the saucepan.
  6. Turn the heat back onto medium-high and add the lemon juice, half of the pasta water, half of the shaved parmeseasn, and chopped parsley.
  7. Mix until the pasta water and infused olive oil emulsify into a sauce (add extra pasta water if needed), add salt and pepper to desired taste.
  8. Serve with the remainder of the parmesean, ENJOY.
