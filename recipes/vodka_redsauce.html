Delicious Vodka Sauce Pasta

Description

Modern vodka sauce with rigatoni.

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 1 box of dried rigatoni
  • 1 cup aged parmesean
  • 4oz yellow onion
  • 4oz cherry tomatoes cut in half
  • 2 oz tomato paste
  • 5 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp red chili flake
  • 2 tsp MSG
  • 4oz chicken stock
  • 2 oz vodka
  • 2 oz heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, preheat a saucepan on medium heat.
  2. Begin boiling pasta, add olive oil to the saucepan until the oil shimmers.
  3. Add yellow onion, garlic, and sliced tomatoes into the pan. Saute until tomatoes are blistered.
  4. Add the chili flake and tomato paste, mix well and cook until the paste begins to brown.
  5. Deglaze with vodka, flambe if desired, once cooked down (30seconds), pour in the chicken stock and reduce.
  6. Once near reduced, add MSG, salt, pepper, and the heavy cream. Mix well.
  7. Once rigatoni is al dente, reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, drain.
  8. Add the cooked pasta, incorporate with half of the reserved pasta water (more if needed), until sauce is emulsified.
  9. Plate up, top with parmesean and as much olive oil as desired. ENJOY.
